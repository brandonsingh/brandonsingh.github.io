Ice Climbing & hiking on glaciers



Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers an easy glacier hike on Sólheimajõrkull glacier with an experience glacier guide abd is available all year around. The tour offers a pick-up from Reyljavik or a meet on location at Sólheimajõrkull glacier. Add even more adventure to your stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing crawling glacier with us.


Sólheimajõrkull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Mýrdalsjõkull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most grand volcanoes ahd during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms of the glacier only adding to the charm of the location. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name Ice-land
